# Ontology of the Derived Modular Spectrum

## What Exists in DMS

In DMS, there are five fundamental entities: the modular spectral functor M, the derived stack X, the derived von Neumann algebra M_X, the modular operator Delta_X, and the derived Dirac operator D_X. Each of these entities has a specific ontological status: whether it is real, abstract, structural, or a combination.
