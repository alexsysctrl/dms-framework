String theory is the extension of quantum field theory where point particles are replaced by one-dimensional strings, providing a unified description of all forces including gravity.

Agent 6 connects DMS to both by showing that:
1. The derived von Neumann algebra M_X encodes quantum gravitational degrees of freedom
