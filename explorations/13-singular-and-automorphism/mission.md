3. **Derived Einstein equation for non-Kähler manifolds with nontrivial torsion** — Agent 4 proved Delta_X = exp(Ric^C + Ric^{(2,0)+(0,2)}) for the Bismut connection with torsion T = d omega. I extend this to manifolds with nontrivial torsion in the Chern connection and prove the torsion correction.

4. **Intersection cohomology of toric varieties** — Agent 4 computed Delta_X for toric varieties. I compute IC_X for P^n, Hirzebruch surfaces, and weighted projective spaces, and relate IC_X to Delta_X.

5. **Full modular automorphism group for non-Kähler manifolds** — Agent 4 proved the modular flow sigma_t = exp(i t Ric(T_X)). I compute the full group Aut(M_X) including outer automorphisms for Hopf surfaces, Inoue surfaces, and Calabi-Eckmann manifolds.
